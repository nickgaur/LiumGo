About LIUM GO:
LIUM GO is a trailblazing leader in the eco-friendly logistics industry, revolutionizing last-mile deliveries with our electric three-wheelers. We're on a mission to create sustainable transportation solutions that leave a positive impact on the environment and communities we serve.


Position Overview:
We are seeking an enthusiastic and dynamic Relationship Manager to join our team. As a Relationship Manager at LIUM GO, you will be responsible for building and nurturing strong partnerships with clients and stakeholders, ensuring seamless communication and exceptional service delivery.


Skills Required:

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong relationship-building abilities
  • Proactive and result-driven attitude
  • Problem-solving skills and customer-centric mindset
  • Proficiency in negotiation and conflict resolution


Key Responsibilities:
Cultivate and maintain strong relationships with clients, vendors, and partners. Understand clients' needs and ensure timely resolution of queries and concerns. Collaborate with internal teams to ensure efficient service delivery and customer satisfaction. Identify growth opportunities and contribute to business development efforts. Provide regular updates and reports to management on relationship status and progress.

Timing: 9 AM to 5 PM (Monday to Friday)
